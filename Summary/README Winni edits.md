## Background

Vitality Bowls Stamford is a first-year franchise. As such, they have a limited marketing budget, limited control regarding digital assets, and franchise brand standards that they must follow. Previously they relied on a third-party agency to manage their digital advertising (i.e. Google, social media) that provided neglible return on investment and no marked differentiation to store revenue. In the Stamford, CT area there’s a growing number of healthy fast-casual dining options. Vitality Bowls Stamford has an opportunity grow its market share.


---

## The Problem

Vitality Bowls Stamford has been open for 8 months. They're located at 1125 High Ridge Road — a high-opportunity wellness corridor surrounded by corporate offices, medical buildings, gyms, and thousands of potential catering customers.

But none of that opportunity is being captured.

The owner took digital advertising back in-house after an agency delivered no measurable results. Currently catering represents less than 5% of revenue despite being listed on EZ Cater — with zero inbound orders. When a catering inquiry does come in, there's no follow-up system. No templates. No sequence. Just a busy owner trying to run a store who hopes the lead doesn't go cold before they can get back to it.

It usually does.

The owner put it simply:

> *"If someone doesn't book on first contact, we likely lose them. No follow-up system exists."*

Two problems needed solving. First, a way to consistently identify the right local audiences, create on-brand content for them, and decide what to boost — without agency support or a significant time investment. Second, a system to identify the best catering prospects in their immediate trade area, reach them consistently, follow up, and convert them into recurring customers.

We built both.

---

## The Solution

A lightweight AI growth engine designed for a first-year franchise owner who doesn't have a marketing team, a big budget, or hours to spend on outreach every week. The whole system runs on one principle: **the owner approves everything before it goes out.** No surprises. No autopilot. Just AI doing the work and the owner making the call.

The tool lives in a single HTML file. No login. No installation. Opens in any browser.

Providing the owner with a system to identify prospects, reach them consistently, follow-up, and convert them into recurring customers.

---

## What's Inside

**Pipeline** is where catering leads live. Every inquiry gets tracked — who they are, how many people, how much it's worth, and what needs to happen next. Click any lead and an AI-drafted response slides up, personalized to that person, ready to review. Hit approve and it sends. A 4-step follow-up sequence schedules automatically from that moment — 48 hours, 7 days, confirmed or closed. The owner never has to remember to follow up again.

**Outreach & Approvals** is the owner's weekly to-do. Five channels — Instagram DM, email, LinkedIn, Google Maps, and Nextdoor — all feeding into one queue. Pending items float to the top with orange number badges. Each card shows who it's going to, why the AI picked them, and a preview of the message. Approve, Skip, or Edit. Estimated time to clear the full queue: 8 minutes.

**Prospect Finder** scans businesses within 5-10 miles of 1125 High Ridge Road and scores them by catering fit. UBS at 97. WWE Corporate HQ at 94. Stamford Health Medical at 92. Each card shows the reasoning, estimated revenue potential, and  recommended outreach channels. Hit **Draft Outreach** and it flows straight into the approval queue. The owner never writes a word from scratch.

**Ad & Content Studio** solves the social media problem directly. Three weekly posts are generated for each of the owner's core audiences — the post-workout crowd, the office lunch segment, and weekend families. All pre-written, all on-brand, ready to approve and post. On the right side, targeted ads for Meta, Google, and Nextdoor sit ready for review — with a budget slider that shows exactly how many Stamford impressions and catering clicks each dollar is expected to deliver. Nothing runs until the owner approves it.

**Phase 2 Roadmap** shows what gets built next — Toast POS integration so walk-in customer data feeds the catering pipeline automatically, Incentivio loyalty rewards so one-time orders become recurring accounts, advanced ad intelligence that scales spend toward what's working, and a review engine that turns their 5.0 Google rating into social proof. The roadmap runs from now through month six, when everything connects into one fully unified system.

## The Numbers That Matter

The potential impact for the owner is not just in added revenue but also giving them more time back in their schedule. The owner defined success clearly. Two to three catering orders per week. One reliable recurring office account would prove the system works. A typical catering order runs $150–400. Getting there from essentially zero is what this tool is built to do — not by adding more complexity to a busy owner's day, but by removing it.

The whole system takes under 30 minutes a week to run. The AI does the thinking. The owner makes the calls.

---

## AI Components

The engine uses natural language generation to draft personalized outreach across five channels, adapting tone and format for each platform. A prospect scoring model ranks local businesses by catering fit based on type, proximity, size, and brand alignment. Content generation produces weekly social captions, hashtags, and CTAs tailored to each audience segment. Follow-up sequencing triggers a four-step cadence automatically on approval. Ad copy generation builds targeted variants for Meta, Google, and Nextdoor with audience recommendations and budget splits included.

---

## How to Run the Demo

Download `vitality_bowls_v6.html` from the `/Code` folder and open it in any browser. No internet required, no login, nothing to install.

Start on the Pipeline tab and hit **Simulate New Inquiry** — that's the moment that shows everything. An Instagram DM comes in, AI drafts a response in four seconds, the owner reviews it and approves. The follow-up sequence schedules itself. That's the whole system in 45 seconds.

From there, work through the tabs in order. Each one has one job and does it clearly.
